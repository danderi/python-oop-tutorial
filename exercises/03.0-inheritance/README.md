## What's Inheritance All About? рџ¤”

Think about your family:
- You might have your mom's eyes or your dad's smile
- You learned some skills from your parents
- You can do everything they can do (like walk and talk)
- Plus, you have your own special talents!

That's exactly how inheritance works in programming! When one class (like a child) inherits from another class (like a parent), it gets all the parent's features plus can add its own special abilities!
